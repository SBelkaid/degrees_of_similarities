Summary

The package "SemEval-2012-Complete-Data-Package" contains all of the data
required for SemEval Task 2. However, you may wish to see the rating numbers
that were used for our two baselines, Random and PMI. This package,
"SemEval-2012-Baselines", contains the data for these two baselines.

Directories

/Random/Testing/  - Random ratings for pairs in the 69 testing categories
/Random/Training/ - Random ratings for pairs in the 10 training categories
/PMI/Testing/     - PMI ratings for pairs in the 69 testing categories
/PMI/Training/    - PMI ratings for pairs in the 10 training categories
